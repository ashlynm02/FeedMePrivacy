Privacy Policy

Introduction

Feed Me is an app that uses Gemini API to generate recipes based on ingredient input. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about our users.

Information Collection

Ingredients entered and used to generate recipes will be collected by Gemini API.

Information Use

Gemini API uses user requests to train their models.

Information Sharing

We do not share any information about our users with any third parties.

Security

We do not store any information about our users.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for anyone 13 or older. Ingredient data is collected by Gemini API.

Changes to this Privacy Policy
